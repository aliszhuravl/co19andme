Simple, Powerful Self-tracking

Self track your symptoms and help those around you and people who are working on solutions to solve this crisis.

We don’t have to be passive onlookers

Everyone wants to get through the pandemic as quickly and safely as possible. But how do we know we’re doing the right thing?

In the age of technology, physical distancing should not keep us from working together. As entrepreneurial people who help companies creatively overcome problems, we didn’t want to just sit and wait for news about our health and families. Given our backgrounds in medicine, IT and engineering, we developed an app, an app that helps you to be part of the solution.

Track

Track your health and possible symptoms over time. Help experts understand this global pandemic in a better way.

Locate

Add the location of your quarantine and get access to a global heat map so you know if you are living in a risk zone or not.

Share

Make your data available for further research and to develop new approaches to help you and the people around you. We're all in this together.
